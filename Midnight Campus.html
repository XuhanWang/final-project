RENOVA

A Time-Profit improvement tool for house renovation

How to enhance house flipping experience?

As an interior designer, I have developed a significant interest in the profits generated by property renovation.

The key to this industry is managing time effectively.

During my work, I have noticed that many of my clients waste substantial amounts of money due to time-related issues.

This has inspired me to come up with the idea of creating an app to assist them.

Location: Toronto

Type: Application Design & User Experience Design

Individual Work

What is house flipping and why does it matter?

House flipping refers to the practice of purchasing a property, typically in need of repairs or renovation, with the intention of selling it quickly for a profit. The process involves acquiring a distressed or undervalued property, making necessary improvements or upgrades, and then reselling it at a higher price.

Flipping Process:

1. Seek for undervalued property

2. Finding Designer & Contractor

5. Resell

3. Buying Material

4. Renovate

Research Conclusion:

1. The most common problem on house flipping is out of controlled time span.

2. Costly repairs are caused by poor workmanship or subpar materials.

3. Overestimating the sale price or underestimating the time it will take to sell can lead to financial losses.

4. Unexpected repairs were the most common issue encountered, followed by budget overruns, delays, and poor-quality work.
