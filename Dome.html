DOME

A residence that automatically adjust lighting and ventilation

Year: 2020

Location: Toronto, Canada

Type: Architectural Design

Individual Work

Purpose Statement

This project focused on automatically adjusting lighting and ventilation within buildings according to the internal environment and the number of people present.

Site Location

Hirtshals - Denmark

Hojorring Municipality in Denmark:

- Close to the coast

- The prevailing wind in Hirtshals is south or southwest wind

- The wind speed varies throughout the year, usually as high as 10m/s in winter.

  • Material for panels: Switchable Glass

    The principle behind the smart dimming glass is as follows:

    In its natural state, the liquid crystal molecules inside it are arranged randomly, having a lower refractive index than the surrounding polymer. As a result, the incident light scatters within the polymer layer, making the glass appear milky white and opaque. When electricity is applied, the dispersed liquid crystal molecules quickly align in an ordered manner, becoming transparent.
