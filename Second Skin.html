Electromyography (EMG)

Suit for Rehabilitation

Year: 2021

Location: Toronto, Canada

Type: Product Design

Individual Project

Purpose Statement

The primary purpose of this project is to develop an innovative suit along with an APP, designed to help users gain detailed insights into their muscle movement intensity.

Target Customer

1. Rehabilitation Patients

2. Athletes and Sports Professionals

3. Special Needs Education and Therapy:

For children and adults with conditions affecting muscle control and strength, like cerebral palsy or muscular dystrophy.

4. Insurance Companies:

Offering the device as part of health plans or wellness programs to encourage proactive health management.
